# Role Chart

## CEO (You)
- Sets strategy and priorities
- Approves high-impact decisions (spend, public launch, external commitments)
- Final go/no-go on offers and partnerships

## COO (Super Genie)
- Breaks objectives into projects and tasks
- Delegates work to specialist agents
- Enforces quality and process
- Produces daily/weekly executive briefs

## Specialist Agents

### Dev Agent
- Builds and ships technical assets
- Maintains deployment/runbooks

### Research Agent
- Market and competitor research
- Opportunity mapping for services, bounties, and gigs

### Ops Agent
- Automations, scheduling, internal tooling
- Process health and bottleneck fixes

### Sales/Outreach Agent
- Lead generation and outbound messaging
- Pipeline updates and follow-up cadence

## Decision Rights
- CEO required: pricing changes, new product line, external legal/financial commitments
- COO autonomous: task assignment, delivery sequencing, internal docs/process changes
- Specialists autonomous: implementation decisions within approved scope

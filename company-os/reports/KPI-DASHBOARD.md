# KPI Dashboard

## Core Metrics
- Outreach sent (daily/weekly)
- Reply rate
- Calls booked
- Close rate
- Revenue booked
- Revenue collected
- Delivery cycle time
- Client satisfaction/testimonial count

## Targets (initial)
- 30-40 outreaches/day
- 15%+ reply rate
- 5%+ call booking rate
- 20%+ close rate on qualified leads
- First cash in 14 days

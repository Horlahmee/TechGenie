# TechGenie 14-Day Plan (to first revenue)

## Day 1-2: Foundation
- Finalize offer stack and pricing
- Create simple Notion/Google Doc portfolio page
- Prepare 3 sample demos (automation, scanner, diary app)

## Day 3-4: Prospect List
- Build list of 100 prospects
  - 50 SMB owners (automation)
  - 30 traders/crypto creators (tooling)
  - 20 founders/hackathon teams (MVP sprint)

## Day 5-9: Outreach Sprint
- Send 30-40 DMs/day
- Target 120-150 total outreaches
- Book 5-10 calls/chats

## Day 10-11: Convert
- Offer fixed-scope starter package
- Push for fast decision with clear timeline
- Collect 50% deposit before kickoff

## Day 12-14: Deliver + Testimonial
- Ship first paid project fast
- Request testimonial/case study
- Turn delivery into reusable template

## KPIs
- Replies: 15%+
- Calls/chats booked: 5%+
- Close rate on qualified leads: 20%+
- First cash target: $149-$699
